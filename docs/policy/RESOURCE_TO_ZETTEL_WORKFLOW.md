# Resource to Zettel Workflow

This defines how information is extracted from `03-Resources` to become concepts in `10-Zettelkasten`.

## 1. Extraction Steps
1. **Identify Claims:** Extract 3-5 core arguments or facts.
2. **Generalize Principles:** Transform project-specific data into reusable "mental models."
3. **Link Discovery:** Find 2-3 existing notes that this resource supports or contradicts.

## 2. Promotion Criteria
- Is the idea **Atomic**? (One idea per note)
- Is the idea **Universal**? (Independent of the project context)
- Is the idea **Non-Redundant**? (Does not already exist in Zettelkasten)

## 3. AI Role
- AI creates a "Digest" section in the Resource note.
- AI suggests a list of new Zettel candidates in the `ZETTEL_PROMOTION_DASHBOARD.md`.
